This app is for use with the WeatherTop 1000:

the state of the art Weather Station with a 5 in 1 Wireless Sensor for Wind Speed & Direction, Rainfall, Temperature and Humidity readings.

WeatherTop brings to you Weather Station with Radio Control Clock ( MSF UK & Ireland ) and 5-in-1 professional sensor that tells you about the different changes in weather outside your space.


This device gets a stylish design and displays , Wind Speed & Direction , Temperature , Humidity & Rain Fall, weather forecast and time-related details .


This weather station gets an LCD display which shows you clear and precise readings. You receive weather data from 5-in-1 professional (YC1129) via 868 MHz RF transmission.


You also get a buzzer alarm clock with ice pre-alert function that beeps when the weather is unfavourable outside.


The entire system is very easy to use and synchronize which makes it a perfect addition to your home.
